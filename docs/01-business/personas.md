## Guest

Can browse, search, view listing detail, view seller profile.

Cannot message, post, save, report, buy packages.

## Buyer

Registered user who can save, like, comment, message, report, and receive notifications.

## Seller

Verified customer who can post listings, upload images, choose packages, receive messages, and promote listings.

## Verified Seller

Seller with phone/ID verification and visible badge.

## Business Seller

Business/dealer/service provider with business verification and higher limits.

## Moderator

Reviews pending listings, reports, messages, comments, images, and reviews.

## Support

Helps users, views support-related account/listing/message info.

## Finance

Views orders, payments, invoices, refunds, provider references.

## Marketing

Manages ads, placements, promotions, campaigns.

## Admin / Super Admin

Manages settings, permissions, users, modules, providers, and system health.
