## Trust Signals

Positive:

- Email verified
- Phone verified
- ID verified
- Business verified
- Account age
- Good reviews
- Low report rate
- Successful listings
- Response rate
- Completed bookings future

Negative:

- Rejected listings
- Many reports
- Suspicious messages
- Duplicate content
- Chargebacks
- Banned linked accounts
- Spam keywords

## Public Trust UI

Show:

- Verified Seller badge
- Business badge
- Rating
- Review count
- Member since
- Response rate
- Seller level

Do not show raw internal fraud score.
