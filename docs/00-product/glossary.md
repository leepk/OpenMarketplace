# Glossary

## Listing

A user-posted marketplace entry. It can be an item, vehicle, property, service, job, or pet.

## Feed

A mixed stream returned by the Feed Engine. It can include listings, featured listings, ads, announcements, promotions, and system cards.

## Promotion

A paid visibility boost applied to a seller's own listing.

## Advertisement

Sponsored inventory/campaign managed separately from listing promotion.

## Media

All uploaded/stored files: listing images, avatars, ad creatives, invoices, message attachments, CMS images, verification docs.

## Commerce

Packages, pricing, orders, checkout, payments, invoices, refunds, coupons, wallet/subscriptions future.

## Trust

Signals that answer whether a user/seller is reliable.

## Engagement

Signals and interactions around content: views, likes, comments, favorites, shares, follows.

## Moderation

Rules and workflows that decide whether content can be published or must be reviewed/rejected.
